ABOUT ME

Jackson Kuffel is a digital marketer, videographer, and writer with a decade of dedicated experience in media production.

Equipped with a dual degree in Business Administration and Television & Film Arts from Buffalo State University, Jackson has expanded his passions for storytelling and people into practiced skills within the retail and technology sectors, in addition to starting his freelance video marketing business, MuseHub.

From market analysis to brand materials, Jackson brings a natural gift for writing and motivated interest in research to each project, even without a visual component involved. No matter what the task is at hand, it is his love for collaboration and relationships that drives Jackson to pursue excellence in every task.
