Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is HireAI?
    HireAI is an AI-powered job description and resume builder that is capable of creating detailed, targeted job descriptions to attract the perfect candidate and curated resumes with a list of recommendations to find the perfect workplace.
  • How much does HireAI cost?
    Creating a job description or resume is free. However, if you would like additional items, such as interview questions, compensation information, and a competency scorecard, those can be accessed by signing up for a subscription.
  • Can I save my job description?
    You sure can! Click the "Copy" button under the job description for easy pasting wherever you would like to post it. This allows you to make any adjustments before sharing it to your candidate pool or posting site.
  • How accurate are the job descriptions?
    The job descriptions generated by HireAI are highly accurate - our tool is constantly learning and improving from new data! However, it is always recommended to review and edit the generated job descriptions to ensure they align with your company's specific needs and branding.
  • What types of jobs can the job description builder be used for?
    HireAI can be used for a wide range of jobs including technical roles, such as software engineers and data scientists, as well as non-technical roles, such as marketing and sales.
