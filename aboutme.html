Jackson Kuffel is a content creator, filmmaker, and student with eight years of dedicated experience in media production. Beginning as a pastime with friends while living in Connecticut, Jackson developed a steady passion for storytelling through visual mediums and incorporated his skills into the various churches at which his dad was pastoring. Upon relocating to Buffalo, Jackson grew into greater leadership roles within the media departments of Restoration Church and Amherst Baptist Church while building his personal skills through freelance media production and acting lessons under the teaching of Frank Rossi.
